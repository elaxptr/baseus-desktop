What's new

01

Animated game toggle

The plain ON/OFF text becomes a sliding pill switch with a rotating accent halo when active — reads as "live / low-latency" at a glance. Flip it on the ANC tab.

02

Living battery rings

Rings sweep and count up from zero on load, carry a soft glow, and the charging bolt pulses. Re-runs each time you open Battery.

03

Spring tab transitions

The active-tab marker glides between icons with a spring curve; panels fade-and-lift in instead of hard-cutting.

04

Micro-interactions

Cards lift on hover, buttons ripple and press, the ANC checkmark pops in, the strength slider expands only when it applies. Ambient accent glow breathes behind the header.

All motion respects prefers-reduced-motion. This is a mockup — the real build ships as SolidJS matching your existing AncTab/HomeTab components. Tell me what to dial up or down.
